RAG process

One source item becomes two different AI products.

A meeting transcript, Teams message, email, or document is synced, cleaned, assigned to a project, stored as raw text, chunked into searchable vectors, then used in two ways: live assistant retrieval and precomputed project intelligence.

The assistant answers live questions from the vector store and tools. Project Intelligence and briefs read compiled evidence cards and synthesized packets.
