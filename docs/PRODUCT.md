# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Construction project managers and executives (CFO, COO) at Alleato Group. PMs use it daily to manage budgets, contracts, change orders, RFIs, submittals, and schedule across active jobs. Executives review financials and forecasts across the project portfolio. Both groups are expert practitioners who know construction management deeply and don't need hand-holding.

## Product Purpose

Alleato PM is a construction project management platform that mirrors Procore's core workflows while being dramatically better to use. It centralizes budgets, commitments, change orders, contracts, RFIs, submittals, directory, schedule, and document management with AI-assisted intelligence layered on top. Success means: every PM can do their daily work faster here than in Procore, and every executive gets a clearer financial picture than a spreadsheet.

## Brand Personality

Polished. Intelligent. Decisive.

The product feels like the Stripe or Linear of construction management: fast, precise, high-density information with zero visual noise. It doesn't look like construction software. It doesn't look like a startup template. It looks like something built by people who take craft seriously.

## Anti-references

- **Procore**: cluttered, enterprise-gray, information overload, feels 10 years old
- **Generic SaaS templates**: purple gradient heroes, icon-card grids, stock photography, rounded everything
- **Construction industry software**: Autodesk, Primavera, CMiC aesthetic, utility-first, no visual hierarchy
- **Heavy analytics dashboards**: Tableau/PowerBI maximalism, charts competing for every pixel, no breathing room

## Design Principles

1. **Data is the design.** Dense, accurate information rendered with precision is more valuable than decoration. Visual polish comes from typography, spacing, and restraint, not from embellishment.

2. **Decisive hierarchy.** Every screen must answer "what matters most" without the user scanning. One primary action, one primary data point, clear secondary tiers. Never ambiguous.

3. **Respect the expert.** These are experienced construction professionals. No onboarding tooltips on day 20, no confirmation dialogs for reversible actions, no success toasts for routine operations. Move fast.

4. **Earned premium.** Polished feel through precision: consistent spacing, exact color roles, crisp type, no off-brand improvisation. Premium is not more decoration; it is less tolerance for sloppiness.

5. **One treatment per pattern.** Every data type, every state, every action has one canonical component. Never two ways to show the same thing.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Keyboard navigable for all interactive elements. Sufficient contrast on all text/background combinations. Status and state communicated through more than color alone (labels, icons, patterns where needed). No reduced-motion exceptions needed at current scope, but Framer Motion animations should respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
