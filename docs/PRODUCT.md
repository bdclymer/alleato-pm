# Alleato PM

register: product

## Product purpose
Help construction program managers, controllers, and financial operators run projects with high-confidence execution data from one place.

## Users
- Project controllers and finance teams who need to track financial controls.
- Project managers coordinating commitments, risks, and compliance.
- Operations staff who keep execution packets and SOP governance accurate.

## Brand and voice
- Clear and operator-first.
- Quiet, professional, and low noise.
- Prioritize decision support and speed over decoration.

## Primary job to do
Surface missing governance and operating procedures quickly so leadership can reduce process risk without searching through scattered folders or chats.

## Success criteria for interfaces
- Fast scanning with low visual friction.
- Minimal, reusable primitives and no decorative wrappers.
- Strong hierarchy for priority, status, ownership, and linkage to source documents.
- Failures and missing states are explicit, not silent.

## Anti references
- Avoid Procore-like chrome-heavy admin layout.
- Avoid playfully branded, consumer-style language.
- Avoid extra cards, wrappers, and duplicate CTAs.
