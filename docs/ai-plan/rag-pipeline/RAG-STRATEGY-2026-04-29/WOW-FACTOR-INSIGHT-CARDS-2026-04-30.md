## Notes For Product Direction

The important part is not the exact wording of these manual cards. The important part is the behavior pattern:

1. Identify the real project or initiative.
2. Pull together recent email, Teams, meeting, and document evidence.
3. Distinguish a raw update from the business implication.
4. Make the system say what should be watched or done next.
5. Preserve source references so the user can audit the reasoning.

This is the difference between a chatbot that summarizes and an assistant that feels like a strategic project partner.
