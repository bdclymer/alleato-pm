02 — Gradients

07 — Voice & Tone

Brand Personality

DeBoer Global communicates with understated authority. The brand voice is confident without being boastful, exclusive without being elitist, and protective without being aggressive.

🛡️
Authoritative
Speak with the quiet confidence of a leader who has protected the world's most high-profile individuals. Let credentials speak through action, not claims.
🤫
Discreet
Never reveal more than necessary. Suggest depth without exposition. The brand's restraint is its greatest luxury signal.
🌍
Global
Reflect the international nature of operations — NYC, London, Abu Dhabi. Sophistication that transcends borders.
🎓
Professional
Precision in language mirrors precision in service. Every word is deliberate, every sentence purposeful. Zero filler.

✓ Do

  • "We protect you and what matters most to you"
  • "Confidentiality, discretion, and protection"
  • "A premier level of protection"
  • "Tailored security solutions"
  • "Proactive approach that makes the difference"

✕ Don't

  • Use exclamation marks or hype language
  • Name-drop clients without authorization
  • Use casual slang or abbreviations
  • Make unsubstantiated superlative claims
  • Use aggressive or militaristic tone
