## Suggested NotebookLM Podcast Prompt

Create an owner-friendly podcast explaining what Alleato AI currently does, the long-term vision for a best-in-class AI-enabled commercial construction firm, and the remaining implementation plan. Keep it practical and business-focused. Explain how this helps protect margin, reduce missed change orders, improve project visibility, automate clerical work, and give Brandon better daily decision intelligence. Avoid deep technical jargon unless it directly affects business value.

## Direction For Tone

- Speak to a construction business owner, not a software engineer.
- Lead with practical business value.
- Explain technical terms only when they affect trust, risk, or implementation sequencing.
- Emphasize that AI prepares evidence and drafts, while humans make the business decision.
- Cover what is built, what remains, and what should happen next.
