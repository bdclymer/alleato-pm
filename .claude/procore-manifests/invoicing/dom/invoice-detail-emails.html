Creating a Purchase Order
Learn how to create purchase orders.

Purchase orders are used to document the types, quantities, and agreed-upon prices for products or services. In the procurement process, a purchase order represents a financial agreement between the buyer (e.g. general contractor) and seller (e.g. subcontractor) to cover the cost of the contract.
