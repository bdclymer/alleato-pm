# Specifications

The Specifications tool organizes the project's specification sections, typically in CSI MasterFormat divisions. Each section can be downloaded, subscribed to for revision notifications, and archived when superseded.

## Open Specifications

1. Select the project.
2. Open **Specifications** from the sidebar.
3. Sections list with division, section number, title, and current revision.

## Browse Sections

- Filter by **Division** (01 General Requirements, 03 Concrete, 23 HVAC, etc.).
- Search by section number or keyword.
- Sort by upload date, division, or revision date.

## Download a Section

1. Open the section.
2. Select **Download** to save the current revision as PDF.

## Subscribe to a Section

Subscribing notifies you when a new revision is uploaded.

1. Open the section.
2. Select **Subscribe**.
3. Notifications appear in your inbox and on the project home page.

## Revisions

When a section is replaced:

- The previous revision moves to **Archived**.
- The new revision becomes current.
- All subscribers receive a notification.

## Archive

Archived sections remain accessible in the **Archive** view. Use this when the project moves through addenda, bulletins, or owner-issued revisions.
