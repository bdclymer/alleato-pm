# Progress Reports

Progress Reports are the periodic status reports issued to the owner or internal stakeholders. The platform helps draft them with AI, routes them through approval, and exports a branded PDF.

## Open Progress Reports

1. Select the project.
2. Open **Progress Reports** from the sidebar, or open the global Progress Reports page to see reports across all projects.

## Generate a Report

1. Select **Create Progress Report**.
2. Choose the **Reporting Period** (week, month, custom).
3. The AI drafts content from recent meetings, daily logs, schedule, RFIs, change events, and budget data.
4. Review each section: executive summary, schedule, budget, RFIs, submittals, change events, photos, look-ahead.
5. Edit sections inline.
6. Save as draft.

## Approval Workflow

- **Draft** — under preparation.
- **Pending Review** — submitted for internal review.
- **Approved** — ready for distribution.
- **Issued** — sent to the owner.

Approval requires the appropriate permission. See [Manage User Access](/help/articles/manage-permissions).

## Export to PDF

1. Open the approved report.
2. Select **Export PDF**.
3. The PDF uses the branded letterhead with cover page, sections, and embedded photos.
4. Download or send via email.
