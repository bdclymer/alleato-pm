# Notifications

Notifications keep you informed about activity that needs your attention. Set your preferences once and adjust them as your role and project portfolio change.

## Open Notification Settings

1. Open the user menu in the top-right corner.
2. Select **Notifications**.

## Notification Types

- **Budget alerts** — line items trending over budget, or variances above a threshold.
- **Deadline reminders** — RFI due dates, submittal due dates, punch item due dates.
- **Approval requests** — invoices, change orders, or progress reports awaiting your approval.
- **Activity mentions** — when someone @mentions you in a comment.
- **Weekly digest** — Monday morning summary across your projects.

## Channels

Choose where each notification type goes:

- **In-app** — bell icon in the header.
- **Email** — sent to your account email.
- **Mobile push** — if the mobile app is installed.

## Mute and Snooze

- Mute a project to stop notifications from that project temporarily.
- Snooze a specific notification thread for a number of hours or days.
