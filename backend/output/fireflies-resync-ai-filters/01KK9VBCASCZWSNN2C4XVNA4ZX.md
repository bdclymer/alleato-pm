## **Timekeeping System Integration**

The team decided to explore implementing Venture’s Workforce Management system to replace manual timekeeping and streamline payroll integration.

- **Adoption of Venture Workforce Management for timekeeping** will automate employee clock-ins and approvals, eliminating manual entry (04:58)
    - Employees can clock in/out via a mobile app or laptop, with geofencing to ensure accurate location tracking.
    - The system supports job-site tracking, allowing employees to assign hours to specific projects or locations.
    - PTO tracking is live and customizable, covering sick leave, vacation, and other categories.
    - Automated data flows directly into payroll, reducing errors and administrative overhead.

- **Approval workflow customization** will enable project managers to approve superintendent time before final approval by Jesse (08:23)
    - PMs, as project owners, will validate work hours, minimizing Jesse’s burden.
    - Jesse will have a consolidated overview to give final approval before submission to payroll.
    - The system allows flexible setup of multiple approvers based on team structure.
    - This approval chain ensures accurate payroll input without redundant manual checks.

- **Full admin control over job and project management** allows internal team members like Maria or Jesse to add or remove jobs anytime after setup (10:38)
    - During implementation, all current jobs will be loaded into the system.
    - Reports are customizable and can be scheduled for automatic delivery, e.g., weekly summaries every Friday morning.
    - This flexibility supports ongoing business growth with multiple active projects.
    - Automated reporting aids in better oversight and workload management.

- **System pricing and implementation timeline** set at $6 per employee monthly plus a flat $350 one-time setup fee (12:44)
    - Implementation is expected to take about **3 to 6 weeks** post-contract signing.
    - If signed by March 20th, the system could be operational by mid-April.

    - The cost structure supports scalable adoption as employee counts fluctuate.
